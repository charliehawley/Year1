How to assemble a skateboard

image

This page will tell you how to put together the only reasonable complete

Hardware

  • A wide-ass deck with a sweet graphic
  • Some hella loose indis
  • Bones reds
  • Polyurethane 56's
  • Griptape - no adverts, you cut and draw your own shit, understand?
  • Hardware - all of it none of this 3 bolt bull, you know where your nose is
  • Sharp knife
  • Skate tool

Assembly

  1. Grip your deck - use your sharpest stanley blade - can be fiddly but for Tony's sake don't bubble or crease, or Coach Frank will have you mop the mess hall
  2. Pierce your hardware through all of that hard work (resist the temptation to practise your trays on the carpet with no trucks and uniterrupted grip)
  3. Line up those indis and bolt them on hard and fast
  4. Pop the reds inside your PU pups - 'oh cool they give you four spares!'
  5. Bolt those bad boys onto the unsheathed arms of your infant stick (don't overtighten)
